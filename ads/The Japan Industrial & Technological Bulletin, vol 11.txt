The Japan Industrial & Technological Bulletin, Vol. 11
Japan External Trade Organization, Machinery and Technology Dept., 1983

	Hattori Seiko Co., Ltd., has developed a wristwatch-type computer
system to be used as portable information equipment apable of handing
personal data.
	Employing the 'Seiko UC-2000 Series' developed by Seiko Instruments
and Electronics Ltd.
...
the system is composed of three components: wristwatch (UC-2000); keyboard
(UC-2100); and controller (UC-2200).
	The wristwatch has the normal functions for time, calendar, alarm and
chronograph, as well as a memo display with capacity 2,000 characters. A
22mm x 17mm liquid crystal panel
